# Browser MCP — Use Cases

A growing gallery of things people have built with Browser MCP. The point: spark ideas for what you can do next.

## Add your own

- **Easy:** [Share a use-case](https://github.com/Agent360dk/browser-mcp/issues/new?template=use-case.yml) — fill the form.
- **Faster:** Ask Claude in your session to "share my browser-mcp use-case" — it knows the format.
- **PR directly:** Edit this file and add your block under **Gallery**.

**What you get:** approved use-cases land here, and the strongest ones get written up as a full page on [browsermcp.dev](https://browsermcp.dev) — crediting you, with a link to your site or company if you'd like one (referral traffic + a page that ranks on your name; outbound links are `rel="nofollow ugc"` as standard).

**One rule:** we showcase legitimate automation on accounts and sites you're authorized to use. Not defeating bot-detection, not accessing things that aren't yours. It keeps the gallery something we can all point to.
